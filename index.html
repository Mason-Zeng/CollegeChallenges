Introduction & Overview

Colleges are often perceived as environments ripe with opportunities for growth and learning. However, they also present significant challenges that students must navigate throughout their academic journey. Specifically, the mental, physical, and financial issues which are very prominent in the students at universities. Baring all of this in mind, hopefully this write up gives us the motivation to make a change so there will be more done to address these issues. A great starting place is our very own university, The Rochester Institute of Technology.
